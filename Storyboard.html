Color Palette

The theme of the website is a soft, feminine aesthetic, and I've incorporated various shades of pastel pink to evoke a delicate and gentle ambiance. The dark purple serves as a striking contrast to the soft pink tones. Pink, as a colour choice, is associated with attributes like softness, femininity, creativity, and a sense of calm.

To achieve a harmonious visual effect, I've employed a monochromatic palette, allowing all elements to seamlessly blend. My primary intention was to create a website that exudes a tranquil and calming atmosphere.

I opted for a minimalistic approach, carefully selecting images that perfectly aligned with the theme. My aim was to incorporate just the right number of visuals, striking a balance that avoided overcrowding and ensured everything seamlessly integrated with the overall design.
